Competitor Research

A short analysis of three competitors and opportunities for RavenClaire.

Competitor Strengths Weaknesses Opportunity for RavenClaire
Competitor A Large marketing budget, many listings Generic service, little personalization Premium personalised service & selective listings
Competitor B Strong online process for buyers Weak offline concierge & bespoke service White-glove in-person service and curated viewings
Competitor C Low fees, fast listing turnaround Lower-quality listings, poor aftercare Focus on high-quality presentation and client care

Conclusions & Recommendations

  1. Differentiate with curated, private listings and concierge viewings.
  2. Invest in listing presentation (professional photography, staging).
  3. Offer bespoke aftercare & investment advice to buyers.
